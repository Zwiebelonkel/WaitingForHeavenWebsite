01 / THE GAME

A bus stop that knows more about you than you do.

In Waiting for Heaven, you sit in an abandoned place and wait. Small interactions, choices, and random events slowly become a silent record of your behavior. In the end, the bus arrives — and takes you to Heaven, Hell, or back into Limbo. 


      

    


    

      
02 / FEATURES


      
What awaits you


      

        

          01
          
Random Bench Events

          

            The longer you sit, the more the world begins to feel wrong:
            red lights, rising water, distant silhouettes, fake sounds, and events that should not happen.
          

        


        

          02
          
Moral Judgement

          

            Your small actions are recorded. Waiting, helping, destroying, ignoring, shooting, donating —
            everything becomes part of your final report.
          

        


        

          03
          
PSX Horror Mood

          

            Low-poly visuals, grain, CRT distortion, scanlines, harsh yellow-black warning tones,
            and the uncomfortable silence before something happens.
          

        


        

          04
          
Interactive Items

          

            Cigarettes, coin, energy drink, flashlight, revolver, pills, notes, adrenaline —
            every item can shift the mood, the world, or your judgement.
